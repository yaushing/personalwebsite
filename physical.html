My Journey
Workouts
Year 2022

With the countinous easing of restrictions significantly increasing the sports I could try, I was excited. I started training in Tennis and whenever allowed tried paddleboarding. This year was also the year of my third National Physical Fitness Assesment (NAPFA) and hence had to train in running, namely endurance training of running a full 2.4km without stopping.

Year 2021

The easing of restrictions significantly increased the sports I could try. Although I will be sitting for my Primary Six Leaving Examination, I alway make sure to get enough exercise.

Year 2020

The COVID19 pandemic has changed how I continue onwards with my journey in fitness and health. I mostly clock 5km of biking or 3km of running around my neighborhood utilizing strava to keep me in check. I have come to appreciate physical fitness as a prerequisite to for the endurance of mental prowess.

Year 2019

2019 was a year where I ventured into various other sporting activity aside from my training in BJJ and swimming, from team based football and badminton to individual focused sports such as figure skating and rock climbing!

Year 2018

I started training in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu (BJJ) as a form of fitness and self defence on top of swimming. I've come to appreaciate that both of these are lifeskills for survival. My most crucial take away from BJJ are the values of compassion, bravery, honesty and humility; values which forms the ethics and moral code of this martial art form.
